**Jean Piaget** (1896–1980) was a Swiss psychologist and the founder of **genetic epistemology** — the study of how knowledge develops. His **Constructivism** held that children are not empty vessels but **active builders of their own understanding**, progressing through stages and adapting via **assimilation** and **accommodation** of **schemas**.

His work directly shaped [**Seymour Papert**](/pages/about/constructionist-education/seymour-papert) (who worked with him in Geneva) and, through Papert's [**Constructionism**](/pages/about/constructionist-education), the idea that learners construct knowledge by acting on their environment.
