# The Palm House, Kew Gardens — Sims recreation

*Thursday, January 8, 2004*

**The Palm House, Kew Gardens, London, England**

A detailed real-world botanical landmark recreated in *The Sims* using community tools including the **Transmogrifier**, credited in the original post to **Persimmon Grove** and **Kiri’s SimThing for Everyone**, with broader context at StrategyPlanet’s Cultural Heritage Foundation hub.

**Historical project page (third-party host; Wayback may vary):**  
http://www.strategyplanet.com/thesims/sas/CulturalHF/PalmHouse/PalmHouseTheme.htm  

The Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew granted permission for the theme; the original article described Kew’s World Heritage recognition and included extensive photography of both the real Palm House and the in-game build.
