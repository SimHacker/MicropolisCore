# Moon Footprint (Apollo rug for The Sims)

*Thursday, January 22, 2004*

**Downloadable Sims object:** Moon Footprint (rug)

**In-game price:** 1000 simoleons

The footprints left by the astronauts in the Sea of Tranquility are more permanent than most solid structures on Earth. Barring a chance meteorite impact, these impressions in the lunar soil will probably last for millions of years. Photographs of the footprints were part of a planned experiment by Aldrin to study the nature of the lunar dust and the effects of pressure on the surface. (NASA photo IDs AS11-40-5880 and AS11-40-5878.)
