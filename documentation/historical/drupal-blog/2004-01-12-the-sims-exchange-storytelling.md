# The Sims Exchange (storytelling and downloadable families)

*Monday, January 12, 2004*

This note pointed readers at EA’s **Sims Exchange**: a specialized community site built around *The Sims* — families, houses, albums, and ranked story content — similar in spirit to Will Wright’s older sketch (referenced in the post) of using **SimCity** as a storytelling medium with hyperlinked neighborhoods.

**Exchange hub (historical link via Wayback when browsing):**  
http://thesims.ea.com/us/exchange  

The original post listed deep links into searches and leaderboards (family albums, houses, “best romances,” random picks, etc.). Those URLs were EA-hosted and change over time; treat them as historical pointers.

The post also embedded a long-running scrapbook-style story teaser (“I Surrender Part IV”) as an example of player-authored serialized fiction attached to Exchange albums.
