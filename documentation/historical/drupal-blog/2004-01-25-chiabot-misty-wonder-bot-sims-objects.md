# ChiaBot and Misty the Wonder Bot (downloadable Sims objects)

*Sunday, January 25, 2004*

Will Wright’s fighting-robot builds, packaged as floor objects for *The Sims*. Original posts listed separate downloadable `.iff` files with preview snapshots on the blog.

## ChiaBot

- **Description:** Will Wright adjusts ChiaBot. (Her lush foliage has been removed for servicing.)
- **In-game price:** 10 simoleons

## Misty the Wonder Bot

- **Description:** Misty the Wonder Bot, with his access panel open. The safety sticker warns you to keep your arms away from the spinning kinetic energy weapon.
- **In-game price:** 10 simoleons

## Inside Misty the Wonder Bot

- **Description:** Inside Misty the Wonder Bot’s access panel is a colorful belly full of high amperage robot guts.
- **In-game price:** 10 simoleons

## Misty the Wonder Bot’s Wheel

- **Description:** A close-up of Misty the Wonder Bot’s wheel and safety warning sticker — a fighting robot built and decorated by Will Wright.
- **In-game price:** 10 simoleons
